Features

Built different.
By design.

Every feature in Binks Browser exists for a reason — to make your browsing faster, safer, and more enjoyable.

Lightweight & Fast

Minimal resource usage means Binks Browser runs smoothly even on older hardware. No unnecessary background processes eating your RAM.

Privacy-First

No telemetry, no data collection, no hidden trackers. Your browsing data stays on your machine — period.

Clean Interface

A clutter-free UI that puts the web front and center. No distracting toolbars or sponsored content — just a clean browsing experience.

Open Source

Fully transparent codebase on GitHub. Inspect it, fork it, contribute to it. Built by the community, for the community.

Tab Management

Efficient tab handling with visual previews. Organize your workflow without the chaos of 50+ open tabs slowing you down.

Smart Search

Quick access to search right from the address bar. Supports multiple search engines so you can pick what works for you.

About

Why Binks?

Binks Browser started as a personal project with a simple goal: build a browser that respects the user. In an era where every major browser is packed with telemetry, sponsored content, and hidden data collection, Binks takes a different approach.

Built with modern technologies and a focus on performance, Binks Browser strips away the unnecessary to deliver what matters most — a fast, clean, and private browsing experience.

Being fully open source under GitHub, anyone can audit the code, report issues, or contribute features. It's a browser shaped by its community, not by corporate interests.
